Request for Unified Communications & Microsoft Teams Phone System

REQUEST FOR QUOTATION Unified Communications & Microsoft Teams Phone System Clear Creek County, Colorado Issuing Department: Clear Creek County IT Department RFQ Number: RFQ #IT-2026-002 Issue Date: June 24, 2026 Submission Deadline: July 8, 2026 at 5:00 PM MDT Submission Method: Email only -- see Section 5 for instructions Contact / Submit To: For security reasons, you must enable JavaScript to view this E-mail address. Award Basis: Best overall value -- price is one factor among several 1. Purpose and Overview Clear Creek County IT Department is soliciting competitive quotations from qualified Unified Communications as a Service (UCaaS) providers for a Microsoft Teams-integrated phone system serving Georgetown, Colorado. This is an open public request; any qualified vendor may respond. The County operates an active Microsoft 365 tenant and seeks a provider to deliver Teams Operator Connect or Direct Routing-based telephony, cloud fax, call recording, SMS/text messaging, and associated hardware and implementation services. This RFQ is not a contract or a commitment to purchase. The County reserves the right to accept or reject any or all quotations, to waive any informalities, and to award to the vendor deemed most advantageous to the County. IMPORTANT: Award Notice -- Value Over Price Alone Vendors will NOT be selected solely on the basis of price. The County will evaluate proposals holistically, considering technical capability, service quality, implementation support, contract flexibility, references, and long-term value. Submitting the lowest price does not guarantee award. 2. Current Environment & Context Vendors should understand the County's existing environment when preparing proposals: - Primary location: Georgetown, CO 80444 - Approximately 233 users / DID lines requiring telephony services - Active Microsoft 365 tenant; Teams is the primary collaboration platform - Approximately 50 mobile users requiring Teams mobile integration - Fax requirements: approximately 10 cloud fax lines + 5 analog / MasterFax-style lines - Call recording required for approximately 3 users (compliance-grade) - SMS/text messaging required for approximately 20 Teams users - 2 conference / meeting room endpoints - Number porting required for approximately 233 US/CAN DIDs 3. Scope of Services Required Vendors must provide a complete solution covering all categories below. Partial proposals will not be considered unless the vendor clearly identifies which items involve third-party subcontracting and names the subcontractor. 3A. Teams Voice -- Operator Connect or Direct Routing - MS Teams Operator Connect (preferred) or Direct Routing -- US/CAN unlimited calling plan - Approximately 230 user seats, unlimited US/CAN inbound and outbound - 2 Resource Account Calling Plans (Auto Attendant / Call Queue) - 2 CT Cloud Voice / Basic SIP lines for non-Teams analog extensions - 3 active US DIDs (additional numbers beyond ported set) 3B. DID / Number Management & E911 - Porting of approximately 233 existing US/CAN telephone numbers - E911 surcharge and dynamic E911 compliance for all active lines - DID assignment professional services for MS Teams tenant configuration 3C. Cloud Fax - 10 cloud fax lines -- inbound and outbound, fax-to-email / email-to-fax - 5 analog / MasterFax-style unlimited lines with ATA adapters 3D. Call Recording - Compliance-grade call recording for approximately 3 users - Must include: unlimited audio retention, playback, tagging, AI transcription/summarization, and advanced admin licenses - Must be compatible with Microsoft Teams Compliance Recording API or equivalent 3E. SMS / Text Messaging for Teams - SMS/MMS standard license for approximately 20 Microsoft Teams users - Must support individual and shared inboxes, group messaging, and Teams-native notifications - 10DLC SMS campaign registration required 3F. Mobile Integration - Mobile background data or Teams mobile dialer solution for approximately 50 users 3G. Meeting Rooms & Webinar - 2 meeting room licenses (Zoom Rooms or Microsoft Teams Rooms equivalent) - Webinar platform supporting up to 1,000 attendees (annual license) 3H. Paging / Overhead Integration - 1 POE multicast paging adapter (e.g., ALGO 8301 or equivalent) for overhead paging integration 3I. Implementation & Professional Services - Full tenant setup, user provisioning, device configuration, and porting management - Auto Attendant and Call Queue configuration - Dedicated implementation project manager throughout deployment - End-user training: minimum 1 live session + post-go-live Q&A + on-demand admin module 4. Evaluation Criteria Proposals will be evaluated by a County review team using the following weighted criteria. Vendors are encouraged to address each area explicitly in their submission. Evaluation Category Weight Notes Technical capability and feature completeness 25% Meets all scope requirements Implementation plan, timeline, and project management 20% Dedicated PM, porting plan Ongoing support model and SLA (24/7 availability, response times) 20% Escalation paths, SLA terms Total cost of ownership (MRC, NRC, equipment, 1-year term) 20% Price is one factor among several References -- government / municipal client experience preferred 10% Provide 2-3 references Contract flexibility, auto-renewal terms, early termination provisions 5% 5.
