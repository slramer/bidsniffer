Howelsen Ice Arena- Second Sheet

The City of Steamboat Springs is seeking a professional design-build contractor to provide comprehensive budget, design, and construction management services for the addition of a second sheet of ice to the existing Howelsen Ice Arena. The addition of the second sheet will allow adequate ice time for all user groups and provide a greatly needed enhancement to one of the city&rsquo;s greatest assets. The funding for this project was secured by private donations and a commitment from the City of Steamboat Springs&rsquo; City Council. Proposals must be submitted via electronic submission on BidNet https://www.bidnetdirect.com/colorado/cityofsteamboatsprings ) no later than 5:00 pm MST , June 15, 2026 as specified on the solicitation notice. Please note that the contractors must register on BidNet ( https://www.bidnetdirect.com/ )
