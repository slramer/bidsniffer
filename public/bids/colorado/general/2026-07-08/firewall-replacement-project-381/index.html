Pricing Options Required: 1-Year and 3-Year subscription terms (both required) 1. Purpose and Overview Clear Creek County IT Department is soliciting competitive quotations from qualified vendors for the procurement and deployment of next-generation firewall (NGFW) hardware as part of a county-wide Firewall Replacement Project. This is an open public request; any qualified vendor may respond. The County is seeking to replace aging firewall infrastructure across multiple sites with Fortinet FortiGate appliances (or approved equivalents). Vendors must provide pricing for hardware and FortiCare/FortiGuard subscription bundles under both a 1-year and a 3-year term so the County may evaluate total cost across both options before making an award decision. This RFQ is not a contract or a commitment to purchase. The County reserves the right to accept or reject any or all quotations, to waive any informalities, and to award to the vendor deemed most advantageous to the County. 2. Current Environment & Context Vendors should understand the County's environment when preparing proposals: - Multiple sites across Clear Creek County, Colorado - Existing firewall infrastructure is end-of-life or approaching end-of-support - Replacing with Fortinet FortiGate appliances at 12 total locations (or approved equivalent) - All appliances must be new -- no refurbished, gray-market, or open-box units - Delivery to: 405 Argentine Street, Georgetown, CO 80444 - County is tax-exempt; exemption certificate will be provided upon award 3. Hardware pricing should be quoted once (it does not change by term); subscription pricing must be quoted separately for each term.
