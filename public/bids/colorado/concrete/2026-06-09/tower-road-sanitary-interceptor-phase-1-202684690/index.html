Tower Road Sanitary Interceptor Phase 1

Phase 1 rehabilitates the Tower Road Sanitary Sewer Interceptor from 59th to 68th Ave, including eight point repairs and 5,974 feet of cured in place (CIPP) lining. Work involves excavations up to 30 feet, manhole and pipe replacement, traffic control, and bypass pumping.
