RFP: Colfax Bus Rapid Transit (BRT) Custodial & Snow Removal Services

Provide custodial services for fourteen (14) BRT stations between Pennsylvania Street and Colorado Boulevard. These services shall include daily station cleaning and trash removal, monthly power washing, on-call services for specific incident clean up, and snow/ice removal during snow events. Services are expected to start in September 2026 and run through December 2027. A Pre-Proposal Meeting will be held on June 15, 2026, at 1:00 p.m. local time. To access the meeting, please call 720-388-6219 and enter the conference ID: 685 928 001# or join via Microsoft Teams . Contractors are responsible for any information conveyed at this meeting.
