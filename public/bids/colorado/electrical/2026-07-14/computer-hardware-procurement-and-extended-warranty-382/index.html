Purpose and Overview The Clear Creek County Sheriff's Office, in coordination with the Clear Creek County IT Department, is soliciting competitive quotations from qualified vendors for the procurement of rugged laptop computers for use in patrol vehicles and field operations. This is an open public request; any qualified vendor may respond. The Sheriff's Office requires high-durability, fully-featured mobile computing platforms suitable for law enforcement patrol environments, including in-vehicle use, outdoor field work, and conditions common to mountain and rural terrain. Units must support 5G cellular connectivity, integrated GPS, and multi-factor authentication including smartcard and fingerprint. This RFQ is not a contract or a commitment to purchase. The County reserves the right to accept or reject any or all quotations, to waive any informalities, and to award to the vendor deemed most advantageous to the County. 2. Deployment Context Vendors should understand the intended operational environment when preparing proposals: - Primary use: Clear Creek County Sheriff's Office patrol vehicles and field assignments - Operating environment: mountain terrain, high altitude, variable weather including snow, rain, and extreme temperature ranges - Units will be used in moving patrol vehicles -- vibration resistance and secure mounting compatibility are essential - Officers require uninterrupted cellular data connectivity via 5G for CAD, RMS, and real-time communications - Integrated GPS is required for AVL / computer-aided dispatch integration - Dual-battery configuration required for extended shift operation without recharging - Full security suite required: smartcard (contact + contactless), fingerprint, and IR camera for facial recognition login - Delivery to: 405 Argentine Street, Georgetown, CO 80444 3. Technical Specifications Vendors must quote the exact model specified below, or may propose a rugged equivalent with full technical documentation. All substitutions must be clearly identified and include a side-by-side specification comparison demonstrating equivalent or superior ruggedness ratings, connectivity, and security features. All units must be new. No refurbished, gray-market, or open-box units will be accepted.
