Emerald Park Irrigation System Replacement Project

Proposals must be submitted via the electronic submission on BidNet ( https://www.bidnetdirect.com/colorado/cityofsteamboatsprings ) no later than 5:00 PM prevailing time on June 18, 2026 The City of Steamboat Springs, hereinafter referred to as the city, is soliciting proposals from qualified contractors to provide professional services for the installation, programming, and service related to a comprehensive irrigation system replacement at Emerald Park in Steamboat Springs, Colorado, hereinafter referred to as the Project (Exhibit A). The contractor will be responsible for removing the existing irrigation system and providing all materials, labor and equipment necessary to install a new irrigation system. The city has prepared an in-house design of the proposed irrigation system which will retain the existing points of connection (POC) to the backflow prevention (BFP) equipment and the raw water supply from the Yampa River pumphouse. The new system will be constructed fully with fused high-density polyethylene (HDPE) mainline and lateral line piping. The new in-ground components will be integrated into the existing Hydropoint/Baseline control platform, hydrometers and additional sensors. The Project&rsquo;s scheduling, phasing, reclamation and timely completion is crucial to minimize conflicts with other active projects in the park and to ensure the park is ready to be reopened in the spring of 2028.
