Paratransit Eligibility Determinations & Travel Training

RTD is issuing this Request for Proposals (RFP) to retain the services of an ADA Mobility Assessment Contractor (AMAC). The AMAC will have the primary task of conducting complementary ADA paratransit eligibility determinations and will also work with Access-a-Ride staff to develop and provide mobility assessment, education, and travel training services for applicants and other customers with disabilities as identified by RTD .
