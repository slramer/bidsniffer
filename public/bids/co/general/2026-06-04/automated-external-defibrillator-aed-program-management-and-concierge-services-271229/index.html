Automated External Defibrillator (AED) Program Management and Concierge Services

Regional Transportation District (RTD) is seeking a qualified service provider to support the management, compliance, and operational readiness of its existing fleet of agency-owned Automated External Defibrillators (AEDs). The selected provider will deliver a secure web-based platform, inspection and expiration tracking, medical oversight, and post-event support to ensure all AED units remain ready for emergency use and aligned with regulatory requirements. This effort will be procured through a competitive process to ensure best value, service reliability, and full transparency in pricing and performance. Single Award or Multi-Award?
