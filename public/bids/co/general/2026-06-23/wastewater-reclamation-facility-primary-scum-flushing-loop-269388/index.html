Wastewater Reclamation Facility Primary Scum Flushing Loop

The Wastewater Reclamation Facility (WRF) is soliciting for a qualified Contractor to provide a new valve and loop system for the Primary Scum Flushing Loop. The project shall generally include Contractor supplying new equipment, removal of existing pipe sections and couplers, and replacement with new pipe sections, couplers, and new valves, to manufacturer specifications to allow the sludge and water through the intake manifold of the Scum Pumps for high volume cleaning and flushing. The contractor shall complete all work as outlined in this Invitation for Bid (IFB).
