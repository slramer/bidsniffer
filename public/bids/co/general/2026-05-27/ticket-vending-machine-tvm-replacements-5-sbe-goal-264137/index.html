Ticket Vending Machine (TVM) Replacements (5% SBE Goal)

The Ticket Vending Machine(s) (TVM) removal and installation work is located within the service area of Regional Transportation District in Denver, Colorado. To support modernization goals and optimize TVM utilization, Contractor shall implement the following changes: - Removal and disposal of 185 existing TVMs - Provide a total of 173 new TVMs including: - 113 new TVMs installed at existing TVM locations - 54 new TVMs along the East Colfax Bus Rapid Transit (BRT) corridor - 1 new TVM at Civic Center Station - 5 new TVM spares for additional parts Option to purchase 6 TVMs for the Denver Union Station Bus Concourse
