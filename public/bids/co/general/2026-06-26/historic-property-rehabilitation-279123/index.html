The City of Wheat Ridge seeks quotes from qualified, responsive, and responsible firms or individuals to rehabilitate wood components including finishing work at the Richards-Hart Estate located on West 28th Avenue and Benton Streets in Wheat Ridge. From laughter-filled family gatherings and heartfelt wedding vows to baby showers and celebrations of life. It's not just for families either! Businesses and nonprofits can also rent the space to host retreats, fundraisers, and special events in a setting that feels like home.
