Wastewater Reclamation Facility (WRF) Switchgear Replacement

In general, this project will consist of Brief Description . The work includes furnishing of all labor, materials, and equipment for the supply, delivery, fabrication, installation, testing, and other materials and services required for the work. Specific features include, but are not limited to: - Replacement of existing fused cutouts on the two incoming 13.2kV utility services with solid blade cutouts (on the City-owned portion of the overhead lines). - Replacement of the plant&rsquo;s 13.2kV metal-enclosed main-tie-main fusible main switchgear lineup with two padmount vacuum fault interrupter switchgear with main-tie-tie-main configuration. - Reconnection and testing of existing incoming and outgoing underground 15kV cables to the new switchgear. - Modification to existing switchgear vault trench covers to accommodate the new switchgear layout. - Development and implementation of protective relay settings to coordinate with the upstream electric utility protection and downstream loads in the WRF. - Provision, installation, and operation of temporary equipment necessary to facilitate the work. Equipment includes but is not limited to standby generator(s) and fuel and associated distribution switchgear, cables, splices, unions, and junctions required to achieve the installation specified herein.
